Night Pairs

Tap a tile to flip it and reveal a gentle night shape. Flip two at a time — if they match, they stay softly glowing. Keep going until you’ve found all six pairs, or stop whenever your mind feels a little softer.

Loop Mosaic

Tap tiles to rotate their soft paths. When every path flows into the next, the whole loop will gently glow.

Dream Fragments

Tap a drifting piece, then tap a glowing slot in the scene to place it. When every fragment rests in its home, the board will softly glow.
