Each game is made to feel like a soft corner for your mind: no hard fails, no rapid tapping, just slow decisions and quiet feedback. Play one, two, or drift through all three.

Drift Tiles
Soft sliding, subtle matches

Gently slide glowing tiles into place to complete quiet patterns. There’s no “wrong” move — pieces simply drift back, inviting you to try a slower path until the board settles into balance.

Short loops No fail state
4–7 calm minutes
Soft Loops
Trace slow, satisfying circles

Connect drifting points into gentle, continuous loops. Lines softly glow as they close, encouraging rhythmic, almost meditative tracing that naturally slows your breathing without asking.

Breath‑friendly pace One‑hand play
6–8 quiet minutes
Gentle Merge
Combine pieces, never punished

Nudge matching shapes together to watch them softly merge and simplify the board. Instead of “game over”, the board just breathes out and resets, ready for as much or as little focus as you have to give.

Pause anytime Easy to resume
5–10 easy minutes
